## About Me

I am a data scientist and applied ML researcher at the University of Missouri-Kansas City, building highly efficient, multi-modal image compression models that address persistent challenges with airborne surveillance systems. I work with [Dr. Zhu Li](https://l.web.umkc.edu/lizhu/) and the Multimedia Computing and Communication lab, under contract with the [Missouri Institute for Defense and Energy](https://info.umkc.edu/mide/).
